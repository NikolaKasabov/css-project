MAGIC MOMENTS IN TIME

From Naomi Campbell’s memorable platform mishap on the runway to Saint Laurent’s style-defining Tribute heels, designer shoes remain at the forefront of the fashion world’s daily gossip and international headline news. Iconic shoes and the women who wore them are littered throughout history – think Audrey Hepburn sporting casual ballet flats in the 1957 film Funny Face or Carrie Bradshaw's closet of colorful and scintillating high heels.

STYLISH SHOES TO LAST A LIFETIME
